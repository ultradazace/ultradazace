CRYPTOCURRENCY

In this page I will show you the basics and what you need to know about. cryptocurrencies and what the over all is.

Cryptocurrencies are currencies online. One can earn them by mining. The way to mine is letting a machine or your computer or GPU to solve a very hard equation. If you have researched, you might have heard ASIC. They are a type of mining equipment and are really powerful. But one asic cannot mine all different types of cryptocurrencies. For example XMR (Monero and a type of cryptocurrency) is asic proof. meaning that ASICs cannot mine them. Some are script mining which means they use a script to mine. For example the script will have if something happens, then do something. Some are just plain trial and error which means sometimes GPU MIGHT be better than ASICS.
